Ministry Of Women Affairs And Poverty Alleviation

The Ministry of Women Affairs and poverty alleviation was established in 1999. Prior to its becoming a full fleged Ministry, it had passed through various stages of evolvement. initially, it used to be a Department of women and Children in Ministry of Youths, sports and Social Welfare. it was later upgraded to a bureau under the Governor's Office. Subsequently, it metamorphosed into Women Commision in 1993 by Decree 42 of 1882.
it eventually became a full-fledged Misistry by virtue of lagos state Official Gazette No 7, vol.34 of 22ND March 2001. Since then, the scope of ita activities has continued to expand to cover a wide spectrum of Sectors in the state.
The ministry establishes and maintains over 20 vocation training centers across the state.
